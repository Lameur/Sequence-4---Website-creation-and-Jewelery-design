Traditionally, the Xinca inhabited areas along the Río Los Esclavos in southeastern Guatemala, extending approximately 50 miles and reaching the border with El Salvador. Today, Xinca communities are primarily found in the departments of Santa Rosa, Jutiapa, and Jalapa. These regions encompass municipalities such as Taxisco, Chiquimulilla, and Guazacapán.
