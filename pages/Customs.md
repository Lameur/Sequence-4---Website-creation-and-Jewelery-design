The Xinca have a variety of customs that reflect their cultural identity. Traditionally, they begin
their day early, rising at 4 in the morning to commence work, and conclude their activities by 5 in
the afternoon. This practice underscores their strong work ethic and connection to the natural
rhythms of their environment. Additionally, the Xinca language, though it has diminished over time,
is a vital component of their cultural heritage. Efforts are underway within the community to
revitalize and preserve their linguistic traditions.
